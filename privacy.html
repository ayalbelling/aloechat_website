Aloe Chat is currently undergoing internal testing. This privacy policy describes the data practices of the Aloe Chat application and service as currently built, and will be updated as the product evolves.

1. Who We Are

Aloe Chat ("we", "us", "our") operates the Aloe Chat mobile application and related backend services (collectively, the "Service"). Aloe Chat is a European-based product. As the operator of the Service, Aloe Chat acts as the data controller for personal data processed through it.

For any privacy-related questions, requests, or concerns, please contact us at: privacy@aloechat.com

2. Scope of This Policy

This policy applies to personal data collected and processed when you download, install, register for, or use the Aloe Chat application on Android or iOS devices. It explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use and store it, and what rights you have over it.

3. Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide and improve the Service. Below is a detailed account of the categories of personal data we process.

3.1 Account & Profile Data

When you create an Aloe Chat account, we collect and store:

  • Your phone number, used for account creation, identity verification, and contact discovery
  • A randomly generated user UUID that identifies your account internally
  • Display name and username that you choose
  • An optional "about" text that you set on your profile
  • Your profile photo, if you choose to upload one, stored alongside a version counter
  • Your discoverability setting, which controls whether other users can find you via contact discovery
  • Your account public key, used for end-to-end encrypted messaging

Some of these fields are stored in encrypted form in our database.

3.2 Device & Authentication Data

To support secure multi-device access and message delivery, we store:

  • A device UUID for each device you register
  • Device type (iOS or Android)
  • Identity keys, signed prekeys, and one-time prekeys used for the Signal-style key exchange that underpins end-to-end encryption
  • An authentication token to keep your session active
  • Last connection timestamp for your device
  • Push notification token (see Section 3.6) associated with each device

3.3 Contact Discovery Data

Aloe Chat can show you which people in your address book are already on the platform. To do this without uploading any of your contacts to our servers, we use a privacy-preserving approach: your app transforms your phone numbers into one-way derived tags (using HMAC-style hashing) and sends just the first part of this tag to the server. Our server sends your device matches to this first part and then your device checks locally if the one of the full tags from the server matches the full tag on your device. Our server cannot see nor figure out who your contacts are if they are not on Aloe Chat.

Discovered Aloe contacts may be cached locally on your device for performance. You can revoke the Contacts permission in your device settings at any time to stop this feature.

3.4 Messages, Posts & Content

Aloe Chat uses end-to-end encryption for direct messages. This means:

  • Message content is encrypted on your device before transmission. We hold only an encrypted payload — we cannot read the content of your messages.
  • Pending message envelopes are stored on our servers only until they are delivered to the recipient's device, after which they are deleted.
  • Posts, comments, and reactions created in the social feed are stored on our servers. The visibility and encryption of social content depends on the audience settings you choose.
  • Group metadata (group identifiers, membership, settings) is stored to manage group messaging.

3.5 Voice & Video Call Data

For voice and video calls, Aloe Chat uses WebRTC, which attempts a direct peer-to-peer connection between participants. When a direct connection is not possible, we use TURN relay servers. For this purpose, we generate and store temporary TURN credentials that expire after each session. We do not record or store the content of your calls.

3.6 Push Notification Tokens

To deliver notifications about incoming messages, calls, and other events, we store push notification tokens issued to your device by Apple (APNs) or Google (FCM). These tokens are associated with your device record and are used solely for notification delivery.

3.7 Pages & Social Features

If you create a Page on Aloe Chat, we store the page's name, description, website URL, handle, page image, and associated roles (e.g., admin status) and engagement data (e.g., reactions).

3.8 Diagnostics & Analytics

To maintain service quality and fix bugs, we collect limited diagnostic and analytics data. This is more detailed than many apps, so we describe it clearly:

  • Error reports: When the app encounters an error, we may log the error type, message, stack trace, platform (iOS/Android), device model, OS version, app version, a device/client identifier, and additional structured context about the error.
  • Analytics events: We record named events (e.g., screens visited, features used) along with a session ID, device platform, device model, app version, timestamps, and a JSON properties object describing the event.
  • Device-session tracking: We maintain a record per device that includes app version, device model, session counts, last-seen timestamp, activity data, and optional internal tags used during testing.

This data is used exclusively to improve the reliability and quality of the Service. It is not used to build advertising profiles and is not shared with third-party analytics companies.

3.9 Data Stored on Your Device

Aloe Chat stores data locally on your device to provide a fast, offline-capable experience:

  • Message history and chats, stored in an encrypted local database (Isar)
  • Encryption keys and session state, stored in your device's secure storage
  • Application settings and preferences
  • A local cache of discovered Aloe contacts

This local data remains on your device and under your control. Uninstalling the app will remove it.

5. How We Use Your Data

We process personal data for the following purposes:

  • Account creation and authentication — to register you, verify your identity, and maintain secure access
  • Contact discovery — to help you find friends and contacts already using Aloe Chat
  • Message delivery — to route and deliver encrypted messages and media to their intended recipients
  • Voice and video calling — to establish and maintain calls between users
  • Push notifications — to alert you to new messages, calls, and events
  • Social features — to display posts, comments, reactions, and Pages
  • Service security and abuse prevention — to detect misuse and protect users and infrastructure
  • Diagnostics and error resolution — to identify and fix bugs and service issues
  • Analytics and improvement — to understand how features are used and improve the product

6. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the United Kingdom, we rely on the following legal bases under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)): Processing necessary to provide the Service you have registered for, including account management, message delivery, calling, and contact discovery.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): Processing for security, fraud prevention, diagnostics, and internal analytics, where these interests are not overridden by your fundamental rights and freedoms.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): For optional permissions such as access to your contacts, camera, microphone, or notification delivery, where you have granted permission via your device settings or an in-app prompt.
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)): Where we are required to process data to comply with applicable law.

7. Third-Party Services

Aloe Chat shares limited data with the following third-party service providers where technically necessary to operate the Service:

  • Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) — used to deliver push notifications to iOS devices. Apple may receive your device push token and notification metadata. See Apple's privacy policy at apple.com/legal/privacy.
  • Google Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) — used to deliver push notifications to Android devices. Google may receive your device push token and notification metadata. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Hosting and infrastructure providers — Aloe Chat's backend services run on cloud infrastructure providers. These providers process data on our behalf under data processing agreements and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

We do not use third-party advertising networks, tracking SDKs, or data brokers, and we do not share your personal data with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your personal data with third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • With infrastructure and service providers strictly necessary to operate the Service (as described in Section 7), under contractual data protection obligations
  • With other Aloe Chat users, to the extent you have chosen to share it (e.g., your display name, profile photo, and posts are visible to users you interact with)
  • Posts you explicitly mark as public may be used by Aloe Chat for marketing and promotional purposes (e.g., featured in app store listings or social media). All other content — private messages, non-public posts, and profile data — is never used for marketing.
  • When required by applicable law, court order, or governmental authority, to the extent we are legally obligated to do so
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, in which case users would be notified in advance

10. Security

We take the security of your data seriously and have implemented a range of technical and organisational measures, including:

  • End-to-end encryption for direct messages, so their content is inaccessible to us or any third party
  • Encryption in transit for all data communicated between your device and our servers (TLS)
  • Encryption at rest for certain sensitive database fields, including account keys and profile data
  • Secure key storage for encryption keys on your device using platform-native secure storage
  • Access controls restricting internal access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

No system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security against all threats. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at privacy@aloechat.com.

11. International Data Transfers

Aloe Chat is European-based and aims to keep data processing within the EEA wherever possible. However, certain third-party services (including Apple APNs and Google FCM) may involve data transfers to the United States or other countries. Where such transfers occur, we ensure they are governed by appropriate safeguards, such as the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent mechanisms.

12. Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable privacy law, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — to receive a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure — to request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Right to restriction — to ask us to limit how we process your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to data portability — to receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object — to object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, to withdraw it at any time (e.g., by revoking device permissions)

You can also exercise in-app controls:

  • Edit your profile information (display name, username, about text, profile photo) at any time in the app
  • Control your discoverability to other users in your profile settings
  • Revoke device permissions (contacts, camera, microphone, notifications) via your device's system settings

To exercise any of the above rights, or to submit a data deletion request, please contact us at privacy@aloechat.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

13. Children's Privacy

Aloe Chat is not intended for use by children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16. If you believe a child under 16 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@aloechat.com and we will take steps to delete that information.

Continued use of the Service after a material change takes effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Previous versions will be made available upon request.
