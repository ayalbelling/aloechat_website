Why Aloe Chat

Privacy isn't a feature. It's the foundation.

We built Aloe Chat because we believe you shouldn't have to trade your personal data for a good messaging experience.

🔐

End-to-End Encryption

Your messages are encrypted on your device before they leave it. We cannot read your conversations — and neither can anyone else.

🇪🇺

Based in Europe

Aloe Chat is European-built and operated under EU law, including GDPR. Your data stays protected by the strongest privacy framework in the world.

🚫

No Ads. No Data Selling.

We will never sell your personal data. Posts you explicitly mark as public may be used for marketing purposes — everything else stays private. Aloe Chat is free and intends to stay that way.

📱

Messaging & Social

Direct messages, group chats, voice and video calls, and a social feed — all in one app, all with privacy at their core.

🔑

Cryptographic Identity

Your account is anchored to public-key cryptography. Aloe uses modern key-exchange protocols to verify identities without a central trust authority.

✂️

Minimal Data Collection

We collect only what's genuinely needed to run the service. We're fully transparent about what that is — read our privacy policy.

Our commitment to your privacy

We believe in full transparency. Read exactly what data Aloe Chat collects and how it's used.
